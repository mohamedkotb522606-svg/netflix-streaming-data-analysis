# 🎬 Netflix Business Analytics Dashboard

> **Turning raw Netflix data into business insights that support smarter content and product decisions.**

---

## 📌 Project Overview
Netflix owns one of the world's largest streaming libraries, but content alone doesn't drive growth. Understanding what users watch, when they watch, and why they stay is what creates a competitive advantage.

This project analyzes Netflix content from a business perspective using **Python** and **Power BI** to uncover patterns in content distribution, audience targeting, ratings, growth trends, and strategic opportunities. 

Instead of focusing only on charts, the project answers real business questions that decision-makers would ask.

---

## 🎯 Business Questions
This project explores and answers the following strategic questions:

### 📑 Content Analysis
* How many Movies and TV Shows are available?
* Which content categories dominate the platform?
* Which countries produce the most Netflix content?

### ⏱ Time Analysis
* How has Netflix's content library grown over time?
* Are Movies or TV Shows increasing faster?
* Which years experienced the highest content growth?

### 👥 Audience Analysis
* Which audience ratings are most common?
* Is Netflix primarily targeting adults or family audiences?

### ⏳ Duration Analysis
* What is the average movie duration?
* How are movie durations distributed?

## 💡 Key Insights
* **Content Mix:** Movies represent the majority of Netflix's catalog compared to TV Shows.
* **Geographical Dominance:** The United States contributes the largest portion of available content globally.
* **Growth Explosion:** Content production and additions accelerated significantly after **2015**.
* **Target Audience:** Mature audience ratings (TV-MA, R) dominate the platform, indicating a strong focus on adult viewers over family content.
* **Talent Concentration:** A relatively small number of directors and actors appear repeatedly across multiple top-performing titles.

---

## 📈 Business Recommendations
Based on the analysis, Netflix could consider the following data-driven strategies:
1. **Optimize Content Spend:** Invest more heavily in the top high-performing genres and content categories.
2. **Global Diversification:** Expand original production partnerships outside dominant countries to capture local markets.
3. **Niche Expansion:** Improve the recommendation engine and content acquisition for underserved family-oriented audiences.
4. **Talent Retention:** Leverage and secure long-term contracts with top-performing directors and actors identified in the data for future tentpole productions.
